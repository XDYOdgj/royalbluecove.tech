MEETWEE Thermal Underwear Set for Women

Engineered for cold-weather performance, MEETWEE thermal underwear combines advanced 3D knitting with a scientifically balanced fabric blend to keep you warm, dry, and confident. The set is crafted with 70% polypropylene, 22% polyamide, and 8% elastane, ensuring efficient moisture-wicking while maintaining a lightweight feel. This makes it the perfect choice for winter sports and outdoor activities where comfort and flexibility are essential.

Featuring 4-way stretch and targeted elasticity, the thermal set moves with your body, offering support during intense workouts and seamless performance. Whether you're on the slopes or training in the gym, its breathable ventilation zones and chafe-free design allow you to focus on your activity without distractions. Designed to be versatile, this base layer is ideal for wearing under activewear or as a standalone piece, making it a must-have for any cold-weather adventure.
