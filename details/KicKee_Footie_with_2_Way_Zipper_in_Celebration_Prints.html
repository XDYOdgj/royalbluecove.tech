Celebrate Every Moment with KicKee Baby Footies

These KicKee footies are a delightful way to dress your little one for every special occasion. With vibrant celebration prints, they add a touch of joy and style to any outfit. Whether it's a birthday, holiday, or just an everyday adventure, these footies are the perfect match to make your baby feel extra special.

Soft and Breathable Bamboo Fabric

made from 95% viscose from bamboo and 5% spandex, these footies offer a comfortable, stretchy fit that's gentle on your baby's sensitive skin. The breathable nature of the fabric ensures your baby stays cozy and comfortable all day long.

Easy Changes, Effortless Style

With a YKK 2-way zipper, these footies make diaper changes quick and simple, so you can spend less time fussing and more time bonding. The zip guard design adds an extra layer of protection, keeping your baby's skin safe and smooth.

Perfect Fit for Every Stage

Available in sizes from preemie to 12 months, these footies provide a classic relaxed fit for younger sizes and a more fitted silhouette for older ones. They're ideal for parents who need convenience without compromising on comfort.

A Complete Matching Set

Pair these footies with a KicKee swaddling blanket or matching PJs to create a cohesively stylish set. It's not just about looking cute — it's about being practical and fuss-free, every step of the way.
